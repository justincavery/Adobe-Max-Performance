Events and Networking

The learning opportunities are only part of what makes MAX amazing. MAX is also the place to interact, get inspired by others in your tribe, make new connections and have some fun. Join us—because these things can only happen when you’re here.

Register

Welcome Reception

Wednesday, November 2

This is the kickoff party for MAX, a dynamic blend of Adobe folks, sponsors and MAX attendees. Mix and mingle and eat and drink. We’re so glad you’re here.

Meet the Teams

Wednesday, November 2

Talk to the Adobe product gurus during the Welcome Reception. Ask them a product question or just stop by to say hi.

MAX Sneaks

Thursday, November 3

Year after year, this is one of MAX’s most popular events. We’ll share a sneak peek at what we’re cooking up in Adobe development labs. Be the first to see what might (or might not) end up in future creative products or services.

MAX Bash

Thursday, November 3

Great food, great entertainment and a rockin' good time. This is the biggest party for MAX and we pull out all the stops to make it amazing. This year Alabama Shakes and The Mowgli's will perform at the Bash.

AIGA San Diego
studio tours

Tuesday, November 1, 2016
6:00 PM - 9:00 PM

AIGA San Diego is partnering with MAX for preconference studio tours in downtown San Diego. Put on your walking shoes and take a tour of five local studios; Mthº, BLVR, Red Door Interactive, BASIC, and Digital Telepathy. At each studio, get a small bite from some of AIGA San Diego's favorite local spots: The Great Maple, The Cravory, Landini's Pizzeria, Ballast Point Brewing Company, Stone Brewery and more. Admission is free for MAX attendees only, but a reservation is required.
