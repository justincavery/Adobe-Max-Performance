Sessions

Sessions, labs, and creativity workshops are where you’ll gain the knowledge and skills you need to stay competitive and the inspiration you need to create your best work.

Sessions run 75 minutes and are generally a presentation followed by Q&A. Labs run ninety minutes and are hands-on technical training on dedicated PCs or Macs preloaded with all the software and files you need. Creativity workshops run ninety minutes to three hours to give you plenty of time to play and explore in an old-school, unplugged hands-on environment.
